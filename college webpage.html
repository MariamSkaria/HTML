About the College

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) is a premier engineering institution located in Kanjirappally, Kerala, India. Established in 2001, the college is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various branches of engineering.

The college provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong emphasis on practical learning.

Library
