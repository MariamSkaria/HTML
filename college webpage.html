About the College

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE) is a premier engineering institution located in Kanjirappally, Kerala, India. Established in 2001, the college is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various branches of engineering.

The college provides state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a strong emphasis on practical learning. It aims to produce engineers who are equipped to face global challenges and contribute to society.

Libraries

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering has a well-stocked central library with thousands of books, journals, and e-resources. The library is designed to cater to the academic and research needs of students and faculty. It also provides access to digital resources like e-books, online journals, and research papers.

  • Central Library - Offers a large collection of technical books, magazines, and periodicals.
  • Digital Library - A modern space with computers and high-speed internet for accessing online resources.
